The Performing Arts Club, commonly known as PAC, is a cultural organization established to provide students with an avenue to develop and refine their talents. The club consists of various departments, including Dance Arts, Vocal Arts, Music Arts, Multimedia, Hosting, Theatre and Drama, and Backstage Management. Since my second semester, I successfully passed the audition to join the Performance Arts Club (PAC). I became an active member of the Drama and Theatre Department, where I participated in two theatre productions and contributed to the management of various programs. In my third semester, I was appointed as an Exco for the Drama and Theatre Department.
